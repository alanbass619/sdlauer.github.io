Theorem: The square of any rational number is also rational.

Proof: Match each justification to the appropriate statement in the correct order.

Statements Statement Step Justification Justification options
  • Let r be a rational number.

  • r2 = (x/y)2 = x2/y2.
  • x2 and y2 are integers,
    y2 ≠ 0.
  • r2 is a rational number.
